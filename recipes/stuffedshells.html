Stuffed Shells

Description

This recipe is for stuffed shells.

Ingredients:

  • 1 16oz. box jumbo shells
  • 2 cups Rao's marinara sauce
  • 1 tsp. lemon zest
  • 16 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • black pepper to taste
  • fresh basil (for garnish)
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tsp. dried oregano

Steps:

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Steam the spinach. Chop the spinach.
  3. Stuff the shells and bake at 425 degrees covered for 20 minutes.
